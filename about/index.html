About Me

In case you didn't know already, I'm Chris. Nice to meet you!

Let's start with what I do in my free time:

I love computers. I've been fascinated with electronics since I was a little kid. Any kind of electronic device you could give me would be glued to my hands for hours on end. My first experience with developing was those little .vbs files people made as joke viruses. I then found out about Windows batch scripting and started doing that. My biggest project (and my proudest) is a batch file that simulated logging on to a computer, creating accounts, and deleting them. I want to continue work on it, but not now. After that, I downloaded Visual Basic Express 2008 to my computer (This was in 2015, by the way) so I could begin more advanced development. Now I have a website on Github, an actively maintained program, and probably much more to come!

I also have a very rich musical background. I (mainly) play guitar, bass, drums, keyboards, and trombone in my school's band. I play in a band outside of school as well.

I run 2 youtube channels, CTECH 9 and ChrisDoesMusic. CTECH 9 is my main channel, I kind of just do whatever I want there. ChrisDoesMusic is dedicated to the music I make. I mainly livestream, because I hate editing videos, but I have a big project coming out for ChrisDoesMusic, so stay tuned!
